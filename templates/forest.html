Safety & Trip Planning

  • Check trail conditions before heading out, especially after stormy weather.
  • Pack water, snacks, a small first-aid kit, and a weather-appropriate layer.
  • Respect wildlife: observe from a distance and never feed animals.
  • Keep to designated trails and leave no trace—carry out what you carry in.
