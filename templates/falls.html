Explore Washington's Beautiful Waterfalls

Popular Waterfalls Near Seattle

Snoqualmie Falls

Location: Snoqualmie, WA (30 minutes from Seattle)

Height: 270 feet

One of the most iconic waterfalls in Washington, Snoqualmie Falls offers stunning views and easy access from a scenic viewpoint.

Rattlesnake Falls

Location: North Bend, WA

Difficulty: Moderate

A scenic 2-mile hike through lush forest leading to a beautiful waterfall with a pristine swimming pool.

Tokul Fall

Location: Snoqualmie, WA

Height: 65 feet

An easy, family-friendly hike with a rewarding view of this picturesque waterfall.

Twin Falls

Location: North Bend, WA

Distance: 3-mile round trip

Experience two consecutive waterfalls connected by a scenic creek, perfect for hiking and photography.
