Popular Lakes Near Seattle

Lake Union

Located in the heart of Seattle, Lake Union offers stunning views, houseboats, and water activities including kayaking and paddleboarding.

Green Lake

A freshwater lake perfect for swimming, walking, and picnicking. The scenic 2.8-mile loop trail is ideal for runners and cyclists.

Lake Washington

Washington's second-largest natural lake, offering beaches, fishing, and beautiful mountain views. Great for families and water sports enthusiasts.

Lake Sammamish

Located east of Seattle, this pristine lake features parks, swimming areas, and is popular for boating and fishing.
